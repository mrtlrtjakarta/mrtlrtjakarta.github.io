SCHEMATIC MAP

A simplified drawing that uses symbols and lines to convey important information and doesn't necessarily represent geography. The most common function of these designs is transport maps. As example of MRT/LRT Jakarta Map, the map will not show all the roads and buildings you may pass along the way. In this case, the entire subway system can be represented as differently colored lines depicting the different subway routes, with dots indicating the stops along the lines.

Unlike the other maps, i also added some 'tourist spots' nearby so users can easily know where to go.
